About Us

Color is everything and the foundation of our company. But colorful people make it really happen. Colorful people like you can make color sing, jump, shout, instigate, enrage, enlighten, inspire. We create the colors but in your hands, you bring them to life and show them to the world. Our collection of colors is basic and fresh, traditional and innovative, subtle and bold. We create the tools for your imagination and craft and adventure. New colors are always on the way. With an extra-long shelf life, our colors are high in solids, give great gloss and have yellowing and uv inhibitors built-in.
Colorful people of the world, these are the instruments of your profession.

5 - Free. Whee.

Dibutyl Phthalate. Formaldehyde. Toluene. Formaldehyde Resin. Camphor.


Nope.


Not in any Misa product. Never. While everyone is entitled to their opinion, our subjective research considers these chemicals to be toxins. And that’s just not how we formulate our lacquers, gels, treatments and preps.
Of course we want durable products that are easy to apply, long-lasting, durable and easy to remove. And we’ve achieved all that without putting you or your clients at risk for exposure to these chemicals.
Getting it right takes some time and experimentation. But it’s all worth it to be free.

Cruelty-Free & Vegan

Shouldn't Everything Be? We bring our dogs into the office and we love to spoil them. It didn’t take much to realize that our love for them should extend to all living things, even those we’ll never meet and especially as a manufacturer.
That’s why Misa Cosmetics has a Vegan and Cruelty-
Free policy in every way possible. Not only are we against animal testing, we pledge to never use animal-derived ingredients in any of our products.
We have selected our suppliers carefully to ensure that they also unfailingly follow this policy. We test Misa products on ourselves, our staff, friendly volunteers and a team of nail techs who have a history using our line. Being Cruelty-Free is not a trend at Misa; we cannot be truly colorful in life without it.
